Main Characters in Knb Description of character Notable Quotes from the Manga/Anime
Kagami Taiga Taiga Kagami is the deuteragonist of the Kuroko no Basuke series as well as the ace and power forward of Seirin High. He is Tetsuya Kuroko's basketball colleague and friend. He is determined to defeat the Generation of Miracles and become the best in Japan. "The reason I like playing basketball is because I can compete with lots of strong opponents!"
Kuroko Tetsuya Tetsuya Kuroko is the main protagonist. He was the Phantom Sixth Player of the Generation of Miracles at Teikō Junior High. Capitalizing on his natural lack of presence, he specialises in misdirection and passes. He now plays as a regular with Seirin with the goal of bringing the team and Taiga Kagami to the top of Japan. "I am a supporting actor, a shadow. But a shadow will become darker if the light is stronger and it will make the white of the light stand out. As the shadow of the main actor, I will make you, the light, the number one in Japan."
Kise Ryota Ryōta Kise was the Generation of Miracles' small forward who now plays at Kaijō High. He is well known for his copying abilities, as he can copy any technique he sees, often with more power than the original. He also works as a model. "I don't care about what's the right argument or whatever right now. What I want is to defeat you. I'm not matured enough like an adult to hold down my instincts and play basketball with logic."
Midorima Shintaro Shintarō Midorima was the vice-captain and shooting guard of the Generation of Miracles. He now plays for one of the Three Kings of Tokyo, Shūtoku High. He is well-known for his amazing ability to shoot a 3-pointer from anywhere he wants, even from the other end of the court. "Man proposes, God disposes. I am a man that proposes, I always carry my lucky item of the day with me as my horoscope said. That's why my shots never miss."
Aomine Daiki Daiki Aomine was the ace player of the Generation of Miracles and was the former partner/light of Kuroko in Teikō. He currently plays as the ace and power forward of Tōō Academy. He and Kagami are sworn rivals after their clashes in the Interhigh preliminaries and in the Winter Cup. He was the main antagonist until the first round of the Winter Cup. "The only one who can beat me, is me."
Murasakibara Atsushi Atsushi Murasakibara was the Generation of Miracles' center. He now plays for Yōsen High. He is staggeringly huge at the height of 208cm and surprisingly fast on the court, but is actually lazy and sluggish in other normal activities. "I'll crush you."
Akashi Sejuro Seijūrō Akashi was the captain of Generation of Miracles. He is the point guard and captain of Rakuzan High and later on for Team Vorpal Swords as well. He is constantly shown with an extremely intimidating and condescending personality. He is the only person able to control the Generation of Miracles as their leader and they all submit to his requests. "My orders are absolute."
